Ocean Freight

We offer Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) shipping solutions across all major global trade lanes. Our partnerships with leading ocean carriers guarantee competitive rates and reliable schedules.

  • ✔ FCL & LCL options
  • ✔ Global port coverage
  • ✔ Real-time cargo tracking
  • ✔ Hazardous goods handling

Air Freight

When time is critical, our air freight services ensure your cargo arrives fast and intact. We work with major airlines to offer express and standard air shipping from Nigeria to destinations worldwide.

  • ✔ Express & standard options
  • ✔ Door-to-door delivery
  • ✔ Temperature-sensitive cargo
  • ✔ 24-hour booking confirmation

Road Haulage

Our modern fleet handles domestic and cross-border road haulage across West Africa. From full truck loads to groupage, we provide flexible and dependable land transport solutions.

  • ✔ Full Truck Load (FTL)
  • ✔ Groupage / LTL
  • ✔ West Africa cross-border
  • ✔ GPS-tracked vehicles
