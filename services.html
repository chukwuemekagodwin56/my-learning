What We Offer

Our Freight & Shipping Services

Comprehensive logistics solutions for businesses of all sizes — from startups to enterprise.

🚢

Sea Freight

We offer FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less than Container Load) services to major ports worldwide. Our partnerships with leading shipping lines ensure competitive rates and reliable schedules.

  • ✔ FCL & LCL shipping
  • ✔ Door-to-port / port-to-door
  • ✔ Hazardous cargo handling
  • ✔ Refrigerated containers
✈️

Air Freight

When speed matters most, our air freight service delivers. We handle express, standard, and charter air cargo with full tracking and documentation support.

  • ✔ Express & standard air cargo
  • ✔ Hand-carry & courier services
  • ✔ Perishables & pharmaceuticals
  • ✔ Next-day options available
🚛

Road Haulage

Our modern fleet covers major routes across West Africa. FTL and LTL options available for domestic and cross-border shipments, with GPS tracking on every truck.

  • ✔ FTL & LTL trucking
  • ✔ Cross-border haulage
  • ✔ Flatbed & specialized trucks
  • ✔ Real-time GPS tracking
📋

Customs & Clearance

Navigate complex customs regulations with our licensed brokers. We handle all documentation, duties, and compliance requirements to ensure smooth cargo release.

  • ✔ Import & export clearance
  • ✔ Duty & tax management
  • ✔ HS code classification
  • ✔ Regulatory compliance
🏭

Warehousing

Secure, climate-controlled warehousing facilities in Lagos and other key locations. Short and long-term storage with full inventory management systems.

  • ✔ Bonded & non-bonded storage
  • ✔ Inventory management
  • ✔ Pick, pack & dispatch
  • ✔ 24/7 security surveillance
📦

Last-Mile Delivery

Get goods from our warehouse or port directly to your customer's doorstep. Reliable, fast last-mile delivery in Lagos and major Nigerian cities.

  • ✔ Same-day & next-day options
  • ✔ E-commerce fulfillment
  • ✔ Proof of delivery (POD)
  • ✔ Returns management
